EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This analysis examines whether the time of day (day vs night)
affects the fatality rate of traffic accidents in Turkey between
2020 and 2024.

The hypothesis tested is:
Traffic accidents occurring at night have higher fatality rates
than those occurring during the day.


HYPOTHESIS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
H2: Traffic accidents that occur at night have a higher fatality rate
compared to those occurring during the day.

METHOD:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Traffic accident data was categorized based on daylight conditions
  (day and night) for each year between 2020 and 2024.

- Fatality rate was calculated as:

  Fatality Rate = Fatalities / Total Accidents

- The fatality rates for day and night were compared using
  a bar chart and numerical analysis.

OBSERVATION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- In every year, night-time fatality rates are higher than daytime rates
- The difference is consistent and significant across all years
- The pattern remains stable over time

RESULT:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The analysis SUPPORTS the hypothesis.

Traffic accidents occurring at night consistently have higher
fatality rates than those occurring during the day.

INTERPRETATION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This result can be explained by several factors:

- Reduced visibility during night-time driving
- Increased driver fatigue and decreased alertness
- Slower reaction times in low-light conditions
- Potentially higher driving speeds at night due to lower traffic density

================================================================================
CONCLUSION:
================================================================================

The findings clearly indicate that time of day is a significant factor
affecting traffic accident severity in Turkey.

Night-time driving presents a higher risk in terms of fatal outcomes
compared to daytime driving.

This suggests that safety measures and awareness efforts should
particularly focus on night-time driving conditions.
